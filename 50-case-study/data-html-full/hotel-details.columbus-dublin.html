        La Quinta Inn Columbus Dublin

        6145 Parkcenter Cir ,
        Dublin, OH 43017-3584
        Phone: 1-614-792-8300
        Fax: 1-614-792-3333

        Add to "My Favorite Hotels" Saved to "My Favorite Hotels"
        Get Driving Directions   |   Things To Do
        The La Quinta Inn Columbus Dublin is nestled in the quiet northwest of Columbus, home of the Dublin Irish Festival and Jack Nicholas's Memorial Tournament, conveniently located off of I-270 at the Tuttle Crossing exit 15. Nearby attractions include The Ohio State University and the new Hollywood Casino Columbus. Enjoy family fun at The Columbus Zoo, the Center Of Science and Industry (COSI), cool off at Zoombezi Bay Water Park, or take your pets for a walk on one of the scenic, riverside walking trails in the area. Reach the Ohio Historical Center, Mott's Military Museum, and the Franklin Park Conservatory in under half an hour. Walk from here to The Mall at Tuttle Crossing, and to great restaurants such as P.F. Chang's, the House of Japan. If you are in town for events at the Columbus Convention Center, Nationwide Arena, and Value City Arena, this is the place to stay. Riverside Methodist and Ohio State University Hospitals, including the Ohio State Veterinary Hospital, are 13 miles.

        Great service and friendly faces are a given at the La Quinta Inn Columbus Dublin, so come relax in our comfortable rooms. Sleep well on our pillow top mattresses or catch your favorite shows in HD on our plug and play flat panel TVs. Log on in your room with our Free Wi-Fi. Enjoy a complimentary Bright Side Breakfast in the morning to start your day off right. And we're pet friendly, so bring your furry friends, too!
