        The La Quinta Inn & Suites Coventry - Providence has been awarded the 2017 TripAdvisor Certificate of Excellence award, and has earned this award consistently over the last 3 years.

        Situated right off I-95 at Exit 7 in a safe and quiet plaza, La Quinta Inn & Suites Coventry/Providence offers you comfort and convenience right in the heart of Rhode Island.

        Our 100% non-smoking hotel is equidistant from bustling downtown Providence, Historic Newport and its mansions as well as South County's beautiful beaches, so you are sure to find excitement in less than a 30 minute drive in all directions. Other local attractions include Roger Williams Park Zoo, Providence Place Mall, Federal Hill, the University of Rhode Island, and the Ryan Center.

        Whether you are traveling for business or leisure, the 15 minute drive to T. F. Green Airport (PVD) guarantees a quick commute, coming or going. Business travelers may take advantage of how close La Quinta Inn & Suites Coventry/Providence is to several major companies including Amgen, Amtrol, GTech, MetLife, and the Quonset Business Park.

        All of our guest rooms at La Quinta Inn & Suites Coventry/Providence come equipped with a fridge, microwave, and personal coffee maker. A 32" HDTV sits in each room across from our signature Bright Morning beds so you may enjoy cable television or one of our free premium movie channels before dozing off to a restful night's sleep. Our guest laundry facility is also available at a nominal fee.

        Relax by the heated indoor pool or get yourself energized in our fitness center, both open year-round. With a brand new 24-hour business kiosk and free high-speed wireless internet throughout the hotel, it's easy to stay connected. Join us each morning for our complimentary hot Brightside Breakfast featuring scrambled eggs, sausage, and waffles. Around-the-clock coffee, tea, and hot chocolate are provided in the lobby to keep you going whenever you're thirsty.

        Our friendly staff is here to meet your needs, whatever they may be, providing you with a smile and the best service you can expect only from La Quinta. Don't forget your pets are welcome, too, at no additional charge; we'll greet them with a treat at the door!
