        Welcome to the newly renovated La Quinta Inn & Suites Walker – Denham Springs hotel, LA! Located near both Baton Rouge and New Orleans, our hotel is the smart choice for access to both metropolitan areas. Our hotel is just east of all of the attractions in Baton Rouge and less than an hour from New Orleans International Airport. Our convenient location allows our guests to enjoy some of the best food, shopping and entertainment Louisiana has to offer. Local attractions include Louisiana State University, the Blue Bayou WaterPark, the famous capitol district, Tiki Tubing , North Park, Greystone Country Club, the Enchanted Mansion, Kidz Korner Playland, and Cajun Lagoon. We are sure our hotel is the hotel for you!

        Our hotel is ideal for both the business and leisure traveler. We provide FREE Wi-Fi, a 24-hour business center with a computer, copy, print and fax machines, and a 24- hour coffee station. We also feature an outdoor pool to help you unwind after a long day of work or play. Before heading out each morning, wake up to a FREE Bright Side Breakfast. Enjoy waffles, breakfast breads, hard-boiled eggs, an assortment of fresh fruits, coffee and juice. Well-fed and well-fueled, you'll be ready to take in the city's rich history by visiting its many museums and historical sites.

        If you are looking for a hotel that provides all the comforts of home, our 100 % smoke free guest rooms offer cable television with HBO and Showtime, as well as mini-refrigerators, coffeemakers, irons, hairdryers and clean, comfortable beds. We also have a guest laundry on site. Interested in cooking out? Step into the gazebo and fire up the grill for an evening of relaxation. Afterwards, visit our fitness center to rejuvenate the body and mind.

        The La Quinta Inn & Suites Walker - Denham Springs hotel caters to both vacationing tourists and business travelers alike. Our staff is ready to welcome you to Louisiana and ensure your stay with us exceeds all expectations!
